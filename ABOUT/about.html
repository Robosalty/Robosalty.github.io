    About Us

    What is Letters to the King?

    "Letters to the King" is a multi-media web story created by ROBO! .

    Plot Summary

    Follow the strange adventures of Erik and Julian as they unravel a small town's unfortunate history with magic. Talking goose included. A Mystery/Coming Of Age story with a dash of humor and tears.
