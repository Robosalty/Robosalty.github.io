    The heavy rain droned out the sound of gravel pressing down into the Earth. Two men wearily approach the lone tombstone, cold biting at their skin ...suffocating humidity consuming their breath. The smaller of the two tilts their flashlight toward the wet stone.

    Perhaps...this will be their last adventure...

    Emily quickly loses interest in the topic and adamantly proceeds to pester Erik on the couch. Letting her grab whatever limb she desired, Erik looks over to Nana sitting in her recliner. The frail woman has already nodded off to sleep, mouth slightly agape. He remembers telling her she didn’t have to stay up with them during their movie nights (knowing someone was very keen to watch hours at a time) but the woman insisted regardless. He didn’t mind but it was starting to make him feel bad having to wake her to go to bed every time.

    As he contemplated washing the dishes left in the sink, his phone chimed, “dinggg”.
