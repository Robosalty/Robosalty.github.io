    The heavy rain droned out the sound of gravel pressing down on the Earth. Two men wearily approach the lone tombstone, cold biting at their skin ...suffocating humidity consuming their breath. The smaller of the two tilts their flashlight toward the wet stone.

    Perhaps...this will be their last adventure!

    Emily quickly loses interest in the topic and adamantly proceeds to pester Erik on the couch. Letting her grab whatever limb she desired, Erik looks at Nana sitting in her recliner. The frail woman has already nodded off to sleep, mouth slightly agape. He remembers telling her she didn’t have to stay up with them during their movie nights (knowing someone was keen to watch hours at a time). She had insisted regardless.

    “Dinggg”. A muffled sound broke through Emily's strange animal groaning noises.





    SCROLL DOWN ⇩







    Erik finishes his nightly routine: Shuffling Nana back to her bed, dragging Emily to hers, washing the dishes, and returning the pillows to the couch. Luckily, everyone in this residence is a heavy sleeper; A few "cling clangs" here and there surely wouldn't disrupt the peace. After a long night, he tosses himself into bed as well and takes another glance at his phone. Erik responds with a simple "Ok." before drifting off to sleep..





    SCROLL DOWN ⇩







    He dreams of a lone figure whose face he couldn't make out in the gloom of Eshore's fog, his mysterious new co-worker. Oddly enough, he wasn't scared of this unknown presence. A dead flashlight dangled at his side. Before Erik could walk any closer, his dream seeped into blankness until sunrise.





    SCROLL DOWN ⇩





    'Things change, huh...'

    Emily walks away giddy, a little pep in her step as she passes through the front gate. Truthfully, she did find the episode last night spooky and crawled into Nana's bed after she was sure Erik had fallen asleep. Even so, she still wanted to watch it again! If her big brother used to like the show, what's stopping him from liking it now?

    'Things change?...then they'll just have to change episodes!'



    END OF PROLOGUE
